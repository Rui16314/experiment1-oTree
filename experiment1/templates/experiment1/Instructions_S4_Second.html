SESSION 4: SECOND-PRICE SEALED BID AUCTION

  • Matching: random each round.
  • Price rule: second-price (winner pays the lower bid).
Payoff rule
  • If your bid < opponent’s bid → you lose and receive 0.
  • If your bid > opponent’s bid → you win and your points are:
    Your points = Your valuation − Your opponent’s bid
  • If the bids are equal → points:
    Your points = (Your valuation − Your opponent’s bid) / 2
