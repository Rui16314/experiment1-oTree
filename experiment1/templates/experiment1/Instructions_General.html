GENERAL INSTRUCTIONS

Welcome to the ECON 3310 Experiment Platform.

The experiment has two parts, each with three sessions. In each session you will play 10 rounds of an auction game (60 rounds total in the whole study). Each round takes ~1 minute.

In every round you receive a private valuation drawn independently and uniformly between 0 and 100 (in cents).

After learning your valuation, you have 1 minute to submit your bid. If you do not submit a bid in time, you receive 0 points for the round.

At the end of the round you will learn whether you won, the winning bid and how many points you earned.
