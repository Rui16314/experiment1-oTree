GENERAL INSTRUCTIONS

Welcome to the ECON 3310 Experiment Platform.

The experiment has two parts, each with three sessions. In each session you will play {{ ROUNDS }} rounds of an auction game.

Participating in this experiment will earn you 100 points.

In every round you receive a private valuation drawn independently and uniformly between 0 and 100.

After learning your valuation, you have 1 minute to submit your bid. If you do not submit a bid in time, you receive 0 points for the round.

At the end of the round you will learn whether you won, the winning bid, and how many points you earned.
