SESSION 6: REPEATED SECOND-PRICE WITH COMMUNICATION

Same as Session 5: you are paired with the same opponent for all {{ ROUNDS_PER_SESSION }} rounds.

New in this session: you may chat with your opponent before submitting your bid. Keep your identity confidential and keep messages on-topic.

  • Matching: fixed — same opponent each round.
  • Communication: chat available before bidding in every round.
  • Price rule: second-price — if you win, you pay your opponent’s bid.
  • Time limit: you have 1 minute to submit a bid each round.
  • Payoff if you win: your valuation − opponent’s bid; otherwise 0.
  • Ties: broken at random unless configured otherwise.
