SESSION 1: FIRST-PRICE SEALED-BID AUCTION

You will play {{ ROUNDS_PER_SESSION }} rounds. At the start of each round you are randomly paired with a different participant.

  • Price rule: first-price — if you win, you pay your own bid.
  • Matching: random — your opponent changes each round.
  • Time limit: you have 1 minute to submit a bid.
  • Payoff if you win: your valuation − your bid. If you lose: 0.
  • Ties: broken at random.
