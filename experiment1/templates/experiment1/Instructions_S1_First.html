SESSION 1: FIRST-PRICE SEALED-BID AUCTION

You will play {{ ROUNDS }} rounds in pairs. At the start of each round you are paired randomly with another participant.

The seller auctions one indivisible item to two buyers. In each round you submit a sealed bid.

Each round your valuation and your opponent’s valuation are drawn independently and uniformly from 0 to 100 (in cents). With probability 1/10,000 your valuation equals 0.01·n for n in 0…10,000.

After learning your valuation, you have 1 minute to submit a bid. You won’t see your opponent’s bid until the round ends, when you also learn whether you won and how many points you earned.

Points in each round depend on

  1. Your bid
  2. Your opponent’s bid
  3. Your valuation

  • If you don’t submit a bid within 1 minute, you get 0 points.
