SESSION 1: FIRST-PRICE SEALED BID AUCTION

  • Matching: random each round (new opponent every round).
  • Price rule: first-price (highest bid pays their own bid).
Payoff rule
  • If your bid < opponent’s bid → you lose and receive 0.
  • If your bid > opponent’s bid → you win and your points are:
    Your points = Your valuation − Your bid
  • If the bids are equal, the winner is chosen at random; payoff is computed as above.
