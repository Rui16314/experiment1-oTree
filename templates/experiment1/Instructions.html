GENERAL INSTRUCTIONS

Welcome to the ECON 3310 Experiment Platform.

This experiment has two parts. Each part includes three separate sessions. In each session, you will complete 10 rounds (60 rounds total). Each round is expected to take about one minute. The whole experiment should take no more than 75 minutes including instructions.

Participating in this experiment will earn you 100 POINTS. You can earn extra points in each round; points from all rounds are added up, so every round matters.

SESSION 1: FIRST-PRICE SEALED BID AUCTION

You will play 10 rounds of this game. At the start of each round, you are paired with another participant (matching method: {{ matching_text }}).

In each round there is an indivisible item. Both of you are interested in buying it. You privately receive a valuation for the item (0–100 points). Your opponent’s valuation is not revealed until the round ends.

How to play each round

  • You have 60 seconds to submit a bid.
  • Price rule: {{ price_rule_text }}.
  • If your bid is higher than your opponent’s, you win and your points are your valuation − the price you pay.
  • If your bid is lower, you lose and earn 0 points.
  • If you don’t submit a bid in time, you earn 0 points.

After each round, you see who won, both bids, both valuations, the price, and your points for the round.
